# E-Commerce Business Intelligence Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📊 Project Overview
This project delivers a comprehensive 4-page Power BI dashboard designed for a growing e-commerce platform. It transforms raw data (10,000+ orders, 1,000+ customers) into actionable business insights to drive revenue growth, optimize inventory, and improve customer retention.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills
* **Data Modeling:** Robust Star Schema with 1:Many relationships.
* **DAX:** Advanced measures for financial and behavioral tracking.
* **Visualization:** Custom KPI cards, Scatter plots for segmentation, and operational Gauge charts.

## 📈 Key Dashboards & Insights
### 1. Executive Summary
*Provides a high-level view of company health.*
* **Key Metric:** Total Revenue reached **$82.59M** with a **48.18%** Profit Margin.

### 2. Customer Deep-Dive
*Focuses on customer lifetime value and churn.*
* **The Killer Insight:** Identified that top-grossing customers (e.g., Shanny and Raina) had a status of **"Churned"**, alerting management to a critical premium customer retention issue.

### 3. Product & Inventory Performance
*Matches profitability with stock levels.*
* **The Killer Insight:** The **Sports** category generated the lowest profit ($5.2M) but held the second-highest inventory levels, indicating overstocking.

### 4. Operations & Returns
*Tracks logistics and order cancellations.*
* **The Killer Insight:** The **8.20% cancellation rate** was evenly distributed across all digital payment methods (Apple Pay, PayPal, Credit Card). This indicates that the cancellation problem is rooted in **post-sale operations and shipping delays**, not customer payment behavior.
